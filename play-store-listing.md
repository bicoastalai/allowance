# Play Store Listing Copy

## App name
Allowance – Daily Budget Tracker

## Short description (80 chars max)
Track your daily spending allowance and build better money habits.

## Full description (4000 chars max)

Allowance gives you a simple number to live by: your daily spending allowance.

Enter your monthly income, recurring bills, and savings goal. Allowance does the math and tells you exactly how much you can spend each day — and shows you your running balance in real time.

**How it works**
Your monthly income minus bills and savings gets divided across the days of the month. Spend under your allowance and your balance grows. Overspend and it shrinks. That's it.

**Track every dollar**
Log expenses in seconds with a category and optional note. Review your full history, edit mistakes, and see exactly where your money goes each month.

**Build a streak**
Every day you stay under your allowance adds to your streak. Watch it grow and get a nudge when you're on a personal best run.

**Savings goals**
Set a goal — emergency fund, new phone, holiday — and log deposits toward it as you save. A live progress bar shows how close you are.

**Stats that matter**
Monthly spending breakdowns by category with mini progress bars. Navigate backward through past months to spot trends.

**Premium – AI Spending Coach**
Upgrade to unlock a personalised AI coach that reads your monthly patterns and gives you a specific, actionable insight every day. Premium also removes all ads.
