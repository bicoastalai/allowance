Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

Allowance ("we", "our", or "us") is a personal budgeting app that helps you track your daily spending against your income. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is stored.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information you provide directly:

  • Account information — your email address, used to create and authenticate your account.
  • Financial information — monthly income, recurring expenses, savings goal percentage, and individual spending transactions that you manually enter. This information is used solely to calculate your daily budget and display your spending history.
  • Savings goals — goal names, target amounts, and deposit records you create within the app.

We do not collect your bank credentials, card numbers, or any information from financial institutions.

2. How We Store Your Data

Your data is stored securely on Supabase (supabase.com), a cloud database provider. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Each user's data is isolated using row-level security so no other user can access your information.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To calculate and display your daily allowance and current balance.
  • To show your spending history, statistics, and savings progress.
  • To generate personalised spending insights via AI (Premium subscribers only). Spending summaries — not raw transaction data — are sent to OpenAI's API to generate insights. OpenAI does not use this data to train its models.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — database and authentication provider. Privacy policy
  • Google AdMob — displays advertisements to free users. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve ads. You can opt out of personalised ads in your device settings. Google Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat — manages in-app subscriptions via Google Play Billing. Privacy policy
  • OpenAI — used server-side to generate AI spending insights for Premium users. Summaries of your spending data are sent to OpenAI. No personally identifiable information (name, email) is included. Privacy policy

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below.

6. Children's Privacy

Allowance is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
