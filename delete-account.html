Delete Your Account

Allowance by Bicoastal AI

You can permanently delete your Allowance account and all associated data at any time. This page explains how.

Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be erased immediately.

What gets deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes:

  • Your email address and login credentials
  • All transactions you have recorded
  • Your monthly income, savings goal, and recurring expenses
  • All savings goals and deposit history
  • Any children or family members you have added
  • All other data associated with your account

How to delete your account in the app

  1. Open the Allowance app on your device.
  2. Tap the Settings tab (bottom navigation).
  3. Scroll to the bottom of the Settings screen.
  4. Tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm by tapping Delete in the confirmation dialog.

Your account and all data will be deleted immediately upon confirmation.

Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us at damonpleslie@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete My Account". Include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 7 days.

Questions

If you have any questions about account deletion or your data, contact us at damonpleslie@gmail.com.
